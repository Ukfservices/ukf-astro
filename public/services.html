Our Complete Service Portfolio

Six specialist services covering every freight requirement for UAE importers and exporters.

Air Freight

Fast, controlled air cargo from Dubai Cargo Village to 200+ global destinations. Priority, express, and standard services for time-sensitive, high-value, and perishable shipments. Direct carrier relationships with Emirates SkyCargo, Etihad Cargo, and all major airlines.

Explore Air Freight

Ocean Freight

Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) services from Jebel Ali to all major global ports. Cost-effective solutions for bulk cargo, with weekly sailings on all key trade lanes including China, Europe, USA, and GCC.

Explore Ocean Freight

Customs Clearance

Licensed customs brokers based at Dubai Cargo Village and Jebel Ali. 100% compliance record across all client shipments. We handle HS code classification, duty assessment, Dubai Trade portal declarations, and all documentation for imports and exports.

Explore Customs Clearance

Road Freight

Flexible and efficient road freight solutions within the UAE and across the GCC. Full Truck Load (FTL) and Less than Truck Load (LTL) options. GPS-tracked vehicles, experienced drivers, and direct door-to-door delivery reducing handling time and cargo damage risk.

Explore Road Freight

Cross-Border Road Services

Seamless cross-border road freight across GCC international borders. Pre-prepared customs documentation ensures drivers arrive at border crossings with everything in order. Regular services to Saudi Arabia, Oman, Kuwait, Qatar, and Bahrain.

Explore Cross-Border

Incoterms Advisory

Expert guidance on Incoterms selection and management for UAE importers and exporters. We manage shipments under EXW, DDP, DDU, FOB, DAP, and C&F  ensuring the right terms are applied for each trade lane, supplier relationship, and risk profile.

Explore Incoterms
